## What is Neru?

**Neru (練る)** — Japanese for _"to refine and master through practice"_ — lets you navigate, click, and scroll anywhere on your screen using only your keyboard. No mouse. No trackpad. No limits.

It's the free, open-source alternative to [Homerow](https://www.homerow.app/), [Mouseless](https://mouseless.click/), and [Wooshy](https://wooshy.app) — with zero paywalls, zero subscriptions, and everything configurable down to the last pixel.

## Key Features

### Three Navigation Modes

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Recursive Grid (Recommended)">
    Divide your screen into a coordinate grid and drill into any region with `u`/`i`/`j`/`k` until you land exactly where you want.
    
    **Why it's recommended:**
    - Works in every app, every window, every corner of macOS
    - No exceptions, no app-specific setup
    - Precise and predictable
    - Never needs tweaking
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Grid">
    Same coordinate-based approach without recursive subdivision. Great for quick coarse navigation when you don't need pixel-perfect precision.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Hints">
    Labels appear on every clickable element for instant accessibility-driven navigation. Requires accessibility tree support, so some apps may need additional configuration.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Direct Mouse Actions

In any navigation mode, trigger mouse actions without switching modes:
- Left click
- Right click
- Double click
- Middle click
- Drag & drop via direct action keybindings

### Vim-Style Scrolling

Scroll anywhere with familiar `j`/`k`, `gg`/`G`, and `Ctrl+D`/`Ctrl+U` bindings — all fully remappable.

### Per-App Exclusions

Disable Neru in specific apps where you don't need it. Perfect for terminal emulators or apps with their own keyboard navigation.

### CLI & Scripting

Full IPC-based CLI lets you control Neru programmatically, integrate with other tools, or build your own automation workflows.

## Platform Support

<Note>
  **Current Status:** Neru is 100% compatible with macOS. Linux and Windows support is in progress and requires community contribution.
</Note>

- **macOS**: ✅ **100% Compatible** - All features fully functional and stable
- **Linux & Windows**: 🔲 **Foundations Ready** - Infrastructure in place, native implementations needed

Neru is architected from the ground up to be cross-platform using a **Hexagonal Architecture (Ports and Adapters)** that isolates OS-specific logic from core business rules.

## Configuration Philosophy

Neru is configured entirely through a single **TOML file** — no GUI required. Every keybinding, every color, every behavior is yours to define.

**Why TOML over a settings UI?**

- Version-control your config in your dotfiles
- Edit faster than clicking through preference panels
- No UI = less code to maintain = more stability
- Shareable, reproducible, scriptable
